CRAP Principles

  • C: The first website has a good use of contrast. There is a purple/blue color scheme where the blue elements highlight important information and buttons. There is also a good use of font-weight to make important articles thicker.
  • R: There is also repetitive use of text bolding, spacing, colors and images.
