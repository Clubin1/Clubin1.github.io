What are Custom Mechanical Keyboards?

Custom Keyboards are personal creations based off of mechanical keyboards. The community creates custom parts that are not industry standard, and the average person can chose parts off the internet to create their own, personal board.

What is a group buy?

A group buy is a community hosted event where a creator sells their designed product wether its keycaps, boards etc, and the community places an order to recieve one. The creator only creates the exact amount that were ordered, so group buy items are only ever created once.
